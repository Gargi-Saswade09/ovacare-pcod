ABOUT PCOD

Understanding Polycystic Ovarian Disease

PCOD affects many women of reproductive age. It is a hormonal condition where the ovaries produce excess immature eggs leading to cysts, hormonal imbalance, and several health issues.

Early detection and lifestyle changes can significantly improve outcomes. OvaCare helps women recognize symptoms, understand their health risk, and take preventive actions.

Backed by awareness-based health guidance
Easy-to-use platform for women’s wellness support
Educational resources for informed health decisions

Key Facts

20% of women worldwide may be affected by PCOD
70% of cases can remain undiagnosed
80% improvement is possible with lifestyle changes
5-10% of women aged 15-44 may experience PCOD-related conditions
